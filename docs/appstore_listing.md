## Description

**Tired of endless feeds, clickbait headlines, and outrage-driven algorithms?**

Today+ reimagines how you consume news. Instead of long articles you never finish, every story is a structured card — headline, key points, and context you can absorb in seconds. Swipe through stories the way you scroll through social media, but actually come away informed.

**Smart recommendations that learn fast.**
Today+ watches what you engage with — not just what you click, but how long you read, what you skip, and what you come back to. Within minutes, your feed starts reflecting your real interests. Love AI and business? You'll see more. Keep skipping celebrity gossip? It fades away. No manual configuration needed.

**What's inside:**
- 12+ topic categories from Tech and Science to Sports and Entertainment
- Deep dive explainers that go beyond the headlines
- Explore page to discover new interests
- Share articles directly with friends
- Save and bookmark for later
- Search across thousands of articles
- Publisher profiles you can follow

**Who it's for:**
Today+ is for curious people who want to stay informed without the anxiety. Students, professionals, anyone who wants signal over noise. If you've ever wished social media taught you something instead of wasting your time — this is it.

No ads. No paywalls. No outrage bait. Just the news that matters to you, structured for understanding.
