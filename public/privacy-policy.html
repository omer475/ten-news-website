Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Today+ ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Today+ mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth with bcrypt hashing).

Usage Data: We collect interaction data to personalize your experience:

  • Articles you view, read, skip, like, or save
  • Time spent reading each article (dwell time)
  • Topics and categories you engage with
  • Search queries within the app
  • Explore page browsing patterns

Device Information: We collect a device identifier (UUID) for guest users to provide personalization without requiring an account.

We do NOT collect:

  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalization: Your reading patterns are used to build a taste profile (a mathematical vector) that helps us recommend articles you're likely to enjoy. This is the core function of the app.
  • Content Improvement: Aggregate (non-individual) engagement data helps us improve article quality and topic coverage.
  • Account Management: Your email is used for authentication, password resets, and important service communications.

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share individual usage data with advertisers
  • Use your data for purposes other than personalization and service improvement
  • Track you across other apps or websites

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS) with the following protections:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • Database access requires authenticated API keys
  • Row-level security policies restrict data access to authorized users

4. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Reading history: Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • Taste vectors: Updated continuously, no historical versions retained
  • Engagement events: Retained for 30 days for personalization, then aggregated and anonymized
  • Search queries: Retained for 7 days on device only

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we hold about you (contact us via email)
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data from Settings > Account > Delete Account
  • Export: Request a copy of your data in machine-readable format
  • Opt out: Use the app as a guest without creating an account (limited personalization)
  • Correct: Update your profile information at any time in Settings

6. Children's Privacy

Today+ is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (privacy policy)
  • Vercel: API hosting (privacy policy)
  • Google Cloud: Content generation pipeline (privacy notice)

We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs (no Firebase Analytics, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude), or tracking pixels.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
